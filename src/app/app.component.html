Lookup Demo

This web app allows you to search for places to live by city and other locations, such as colleges or other points of interest.
You can use this app to find a new place to live, or to explore different neighborhoods in your current city.

Simply enter the city you would like to live in in the search bar.
You can also specify locations you would like to live by, such as a college or a point of interest.
For example, you could search for "houses near Stanford University" or "Apartments in downtown Seattle."
